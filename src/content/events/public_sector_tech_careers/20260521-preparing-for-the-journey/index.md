​Graduating from university and entering the workforce may be an exciting and intimidating experience for many. This event aims to help prepare for a career in a technical role, and show how the public service could be a part of that.

​This event is intended for people in their final year of university, or have graduated in the last two years, pursuing the following roles:

-   ​Software Engineering
    
-   ​Data Science
    
-   ​AI Engineering
    
-   ​Cybersecurity
    

​Recruiters from the following agencies will be present at the event:

-   ​Digital Intelligence Service (DIS)
    
-   ​Home Team Science and Technology Agency (HTX)
    

​Talk topics include:

-   ​Preparing for the Technical Interview - student speakers from NUS SoC TIPS \[Technical Interview Preparation (Summer)\]
    
-   ​How It Started vs How It's Going: a person's experience with the HTX Associate Programme
    
-   ​Why join the Digital and Intelligence Service (DIS): Defending Singapore in the Digital Domain
    

​Expect a thoughtful and focused conversation with practical perspective and guidance you can carry forward after the event. It is a chance to pause, ask better questions, and leave with a stronger sense of readiness for the road ahead.

​Please note that due to the agencies involved, priority will be given to Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents.
