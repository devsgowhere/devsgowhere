🧱 **10.30am - 11.10am : Topic - Introduction to Claude Cowork**

Claude Cowork is Anthropic's AI agent designed for non-developers. Unlike a vanilla chatbot that answers one question at a time, Cowork can take on entire tasks through direct interaction with your file system, browser, and external tools like Slack, Notion, and Google Drive. Cowork's capabilities are extendable through Claude Skills, which are reusable capabilities that can turn Claude into a specialist. In this talk, we'll walk through what Cowork can do and explore real-world use cases across different functions.

🤖 **11.30am - 12.10pm : Topic - From Meeting to Prototype: Turning Conversations into Working Solutions with AI**

What if every meeting could instantly turn into not just tasks—but a working prototype? This demo showcases a working AI-driven prototype that transforms a recorded session into structured outputs— capturing key points, organizing them into requirements, identifying gaps, and generating ready-to-use tasks in Jira. Going a step further, the solution automatically creates a visual prototype based on those requirements, allowing teams to quickly see and validate ideas before development begins. Learn how to refine AI-generated outputs due to limitations of the prototype in addressing meeting nuances. By removing manual documentation and accelerating the journey from discussion to tangible output, this approach helps teams move faster, align better, and bring ideas to life in minutes.
