**About this Meetup**
Gear up for ***STACK Meetup [2.0]*** – our upcoming revamp of STACK Meetups! Join us for our first pilot session, in collaboration with ***GovTech’s Data Practice***, where we'll dive into the topic of ***data quality***, the foundation of trust in today’s data-driven world.

Alongside experts from both the private and public sectors, we'll explore how data challenges are tackled, and the strategies used to ensure data quality in generating reliable insights. Hear from our speakers’ firsthand experiences, and learn key practices that can help manage, safeguard, and unlock the full potential of your data.

If you work with data in your role, don’t miss out on this exciting opportunity to learn, share, and be part of our fresh new direction in building ***a specialised data community within STACK Community***. Join us today. Seats are limited so **[sign up](https://go.gov.sg/stackmeetup-25nov2025-reg/) now!**
