​Join us for an evening at Lorong AI (at One North) where we dig into the question at the center of AI’s next chapter: how do we build systems that continuously learn from the world they serve, not just blindly scale compute?

​Sara Hooker, Co-founder, of Adaption will sit down with Dr. Leslie Teo, Senior Director of AI Products, AI Singapore & SEA-LION.

​Sara Hooker and the Adaption team are building AI systems that continuously learn and adapt on the fly, without the costly retraining cycles the industry has come to accept as normal. Dr. Teo and his team built SEA-LION, the open-source LLM family designed to better understand Southeast Asia's languages and communities. Together they'll get into what efficient, adaptive AI looks like when you're building for the real world, not just benchmarks.

​This is a space for practitioners, researchers, builders, and the deeply curious. Come with questions and stay for the conversation.
