Vibe coding and AI-assisted prototyping allow us to build faster than ever before. But what happens after the prototype works on your machine? Bridging the massive gap between a generated script and a production-ready application requires structure. In this 3-hour hands-on workshop, we will transition from rapid prototyping to sustainable engineering. You will learn how to tame AI-generated codebases, master the Gemini CLI for feature development and debugging, establish automated testing and CI/CD pipelines, and securely deploy your application to Google Cloud.
